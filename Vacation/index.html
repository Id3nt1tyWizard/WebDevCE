Welcome to Gray's Ski Recs

Discover Your Next Powder Paradise

For over 15 years, Budget Vacations has been connecting adventure seekers with the world's most incredible ski destinations—without the luxury price tag. I believe that epic powder days, breathtaking mountain views, and unforgettable alpine experiences shouldn't be reserved for the ultra-wealthy.

Who I Serve

I specialize in crafting affordable ski adventures for thrill-seekers, families, and groups who want authentic mountain experiences. Whether you're a seasoned shredder looking for untouched backcountry or a beginner ready to tackle your first green run, I'll find the perfect slope for your skill level and budget.

Hidden Gems, Big Adventures

Forget the overpriced mega-resorts. I've spent years uncovering hidden gem ski towns and under-the-radar mountain destinations across North America, Europe, and beyond. From charming Slovenian villages to affordable Japanese powder towns, I'll show you where locals actually ski—places with shorter lift lines, cheaper lift tickets, and way more character.
